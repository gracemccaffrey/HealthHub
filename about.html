About Us

What is Our Goal?

Our goal is to improve health literacy and access to medical care for underserved communities across America. Many individuals face difficulties in understanding healthcare options, navigating insurance, and finding the right providers. We aim to simplify this process by offering a platform that provides clear, accessible information on medical services, common healthcare terminology, and frequently asked questions. By empowering individuals with the knowledge they need, we hope to make healthcare more approachable and ensure everyone can make informed decisions about their well-being.

Who Are We?

We are a group of students from The Ohio State University, enrolled in ECE 5050: Humanitarian Engineering in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. Our team is passionate about leveraging technology to create impactful solutions that address real-world challenges. Through this project, we aim to bridge the gap between healthcare services and the communities that need them most by developing an accessible and user-friendly health literacy platform.
